About Hungry Horrors

Hungry Horrors is a unique roguelite deckbuilder that flips the script on traditional monster encounters. Instead of battling creatures with swords and spells, you'll appease them with delicious traditional dishes from British and Irish cuisine.

Each monster you encounter has distinct food preferences rooted in actual mythology and regional traditions. Serve the right dish, and they'll be satisfied. Serve the wrong one, and well... you'll become the meal.

Build your deck of recipes, manage your ingredients carefully, and master the art of monstrous hospitality. With each run, unlock new dishes, discover creature preferences, and expand your culinary arsenal.

Developed by Clumsy Bear Studio, Hungry Horrors features hand-pixelled art and a bespoke soundtrack that perfectly captures the eerie yet whimsical atmosphere of folklore-inspired cooking adventures.
